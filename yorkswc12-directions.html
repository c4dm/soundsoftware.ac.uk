York2012 Bootcamp: Venue location and directions

Venue Location

The workshop will take place in the University of York, on the Law and Management building, room LMB/031, on the Heslington East Campus.

Getting there

Please refer to the University of York's page for detailed information and maps:

  • Maps and directions

Buses within York

If you're are staying in York and making their way to campus by public transport, then you should catch either the number 44 or 4 bus service (these buses run approx. every 10-minutes):

  • Bus route for the no.4 service can be found on the First York website: http://www.firstgroup.com/ukbus/york/
    This service runs through the centre of York from Blossom Street to the Railway Station, then to Rougier Street, Clifford Street, Paragon Street, Heslington Road, and then makes its way to the University going first through Campus West and then up to Campus East.
  • The bus route for the no.44 service can be found on the TransDev website: http://www.yorkbus.co.uk/ and clicking on the 'Unibus' tab. This service goes a slightly different way through the centre of York to the no.4 service.

By Car

If you are travelling to campus by car, please take notice that although there is a car park at Heslington East campus, it is small and, on a weekday, it does get full quite quickly; weekends aren't as busy though.

Should you get to Heslington East car park and it is full, you will need to drive to main campus (Heslington West) and park in one of the Pay & Display car parks dotted around campus (around Â£6 per day for the Thursday and Friday which you will need to pay as soon as you have parked; Saturday is free parking).

Campus Central car park, on University Road, would be the best alternative car park, and the you would then need to cross the road by the bridge and catch either the free 44 or 4 bus service direct to Campus East.

These buses run approx. every 10-minutes. The number 4 service drop off by the car park at Heslington East, and the number 44 drop off outside the main Ron Cooke Hub entrance. You should then follow the signs marked 'York Law School' and 'York Management School' which will take you to the Law & Management Building.

If you manage to find a space in the Heslington East car park this is on a barrier system, and payment should be made on exiting.

SoundSoftware 2014: Videos now available!

The SoundSoftware 2014 workshop, our third annual workshop on software and data in audio and music research, was just as enjoyable as the previous two. Because so much research in this field ends up being expressed through software, a software workshop turns out to be all about the means by which research becomes useful and relevant to people other than the original researchers—fertile ground for interesting and thought-provoking talks.

The workshop videos are now available online at http://soundsoftware.ac.uk/soundsoftware2014, so if you weren't able to make it in person, catch up here!

Register now for the SoundSoftware Third Workshop!

Our third annual one-day workshop on Software and Data for Audio and Music Research takes place on the 8th of July 2014 at Queen Mary, University of London.
